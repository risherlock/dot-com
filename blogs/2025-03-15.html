Writing

Sriza
March 15, 2025

The best thing about writing to let go your thoughts is you can come back to it as you scroll down your timeline
And the worst thing about writing to let your thoughts is you can come back to it as you scroll down your timeline
And somewhere in silence the echo of voices sinks, delayed by ages, renewed by new wounds
And sometimes in decades, your numbness fades away, and the dust that scattered above your vision vapours away
And the moments on which those words hit the page, reverbs as sadness, thin and spooky as ghost
Disgusted by your being, you cry to wash your clowns, desperate to throw all the times you let yourself down.
