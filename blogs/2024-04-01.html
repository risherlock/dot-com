Finite-state machine and its implementation

In this blog post we start with a practical engineering problem and attempt to model, and implement it using the ideas of finite-state machine. Furthermore, we look into the mathematical model of FSM and discuss its implications to our problem. Since FSM is standardized model of computation, it allows us to perform memory and space analysis of our algorithm while ignoring many implementation details. In terms of software implementation, its like having a template and all we need to do is to fill in the details of particular problem at hand. It is analogous mathematical modelling a dynamical system using differential equations and using the tools and techniques of differential equations to analyse the stability and perform numerical simulations of the system. We need not reinvent techniques for each dynamical system but use the ones that are already available and thats pretty much same with the idea of formulating our problem in FSM.
