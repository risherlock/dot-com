Two lives of the Count of Skitville

Preface

Lord Langsley of Skitville is no less then a god in his province. His deeds of good and philanthropic works, which is so well known in his land, need not be recounted. When going through his legal papers, his nephew found a manuscript that shed a new light on whe he actually was. It is of surprise to all who knew him on how so well known before he resided on Skitville. We have presented his manuscript as it is with some minor grammatical edits and correction of date. We believe it will come as shock on who he was.

Skitville

I have lived a long life. So long that I lived twice. Shortly after my reception of King's Police Medal, my poor father died leaving his empire to my care. I had never been able to make him a proud father in account of me not fulfilling his wish of being heir to his empire. Instead I joined police force. I never dearmed of continuing his legacy for he was noblest of men. I wanted to serve my nation. However, things had changed a lot. I have meet people and experienced things which made it clear to me that I would indeed join Skitville and join his leagcy.

I returned to my land as Lord Skitville.

Inspector
